Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

By Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviere family the atrist painted that year.

  • Date: 1806
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Dimensions: 100cm x 70cm
  • Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
